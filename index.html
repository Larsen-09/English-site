Scotland

Nemo me impune lasseci

Scotland is a European country located in the British Isles, it is the northernmost point of the British Isles and is part of the United Kingdom together with England, Wales and Northern Ireland. Scotland was originally established as a kingdom in 843CE by Kenneth MacAlpin who conquered the Picts and become the first king of Scotland.

Scotland is known for many things, like their culture, intriguing history, and maybe above all else their highly unique accent. Scotland is also a place of great natural beauty, see the Scottish Highlands as an example. Scotland also has a rich architectural history. As there are still well over 2000 standing castles spread throughout Scotland.

Coming in with 5.5 million citizens and a size of 77 910 km² Scotland is not an especially large country by any means. But it becomes a lot larger when we look at it toghether with the rest of the UK. We can see they have a population of approximately 69 million with a landmass of 243 610 km².
