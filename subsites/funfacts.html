Fun facts

Fun facts about scotland

The unicorn.

Scotlands national animal is the unicorn. Which they chose as a symbol of purity, innocence, power and independance. First being used on the coat of arms in the 12th century.

The fire brigade.

The Edinburgh fire brigade was the world's first fire brigade to be paid for by the city, and to be free for the public

Golf.

Modern golf, and the way we see and play it were defined by scotland back in the middle ages.


Haggis

Haggis is the national dish of Scotland. consisting of several sheep organs aswell as vegetables being minced and cooked encased in an animal's stomach.

The raincoat

The first raincoat was made in scotland in 1824 by the scottish chemist Charles Macintosh

Redheads.

Scotland is the country with the highest amount of rehead citizens. With around 13% of the population being born with red hair.
